Marketing & Community Reserve
5% of Total Supply - Strategic Growth Allocation

A dedicated 5% of the total GHOST token supply has been strategically allocated and secured in a time-locked smart contract for marketing initiatives and community development programs. This allocation ensures sustainable growth and community engagement over the project's critical early phases.

Vesting Schedule & Release Mechanism
12-Month Lock Period with Quarterly Unlocks
