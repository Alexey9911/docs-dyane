## Developer Allocation

**10% of Total Supply - Team & Development Reserve**

The Ghost Link development team has been allocated 10% of the total $GLINK token supply to ensure continued development, maintenance, and innovation of the privacy protocol.

## Vesting Schedule & Release Mechanism

**Quarterly Unlock Schedule Starting at Launch**

| Parameter              | Details                                        |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Total Dev Allocation   | 10% of total supply                            |
| Initial Unlock         | 2.5% of total supply (available at TGE)       |
| Remaining Amount       | 7.5% of total supply                           |
| Unlock Schedule        | 2.5% released every 3 months                  |
| Total Vesting Duration | 9 months (3 quarterly unlocks)                |
| Smart Contract Secured | All locked tokens held in time-lock contracts |

## Quarterly Release Breakdown

| Timeline | Release                | % of Total Supply | Status        |
