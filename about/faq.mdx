## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="What is Ghost Link?">
    Ghost Link is a zero-knowledge payment module on Solana that enables private transfers of SOL, USDC, and USDT without exposing wallet connections or transaction history.
  </AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="How does Ghost Link protect my privacy?">
    Ghost Link uses a multi-layered privacy architecture: zero-knowledge proofs validate transactions without revealing sensitive data, temporary wallet generation breaks on-chain links between sender and recipient, and cryptographic mixing obscures transaction patterns and amounts.
  </AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="Is Ghost Link only for SOL?">
    No. Ghost Link supports private transfers across **SOL**, **USDC**, and **USDT**. All assets benefit from the same zero-knowledge privacy protection.
  </AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="Is Ghost Link open source?">
    Ghost Link is built in public with transparency and community feedback. Follow our official channels for the latest updates on development.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

### Features

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="What is Send?">
    Send is Ghost Link's core feature for private one-to-one transfers with zero traceability. Perfect for confidential payments and personal transfers where privacy is paramount.
  </AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="What is Distribute?">
    Distribute allows you to fund multiple wallets privately in bulk. Ideal for airdrops, payroll, and mass payments while maintaining complete privacy for up to 50 wallets per batch.
  </AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="What is Sweep?">
    Sweep is a wallet consolidation tool that lets you recover funds from multiple wallets into a single destination. Perfect for recovering SOL from old wallets, bot wallets, or batch-generated wallets.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

### Fees & Token

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="What are the fees?">
    Ghost Link charges a **1% service fee** on transactions. Network security fees and system gas fees also apply. All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm any transaction.
  </AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="How do I waive service fees?">
    Hold **$GLINK tokens** (0.5+ tokens) to get complete service fee exemption. Only base protocol and system fees apply for token holders.
  </AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="Where can I get the $GLINK token?">
    Stay tuned on our [Twitter/X](https://x.com/GhostLinkFi) and [Telegram](https://t.me/GhostLinkFinance) for the official launch announcement and token availability.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

### Security

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Is my data safe?">
    All sensitive data is encrypted client-side in your browser. Ghost Link never stores your private keys or recovery information on any server. Backup keys are generated locally and remain under your control.
  </AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="What happens if a transaction fails?">
    Unused fees are automatically returned to your wallet. If a distribution is interrupted, you can resume it using the session management system and your exported private keys.
  </AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="Can I recover my funds?">
    Yes. Each transaction provides a backup recovery key for the temporary wallet. Keep this key safe — it's the only way to recover funds if something goes wrong during processing.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>
