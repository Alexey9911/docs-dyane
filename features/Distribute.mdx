## Fund Multiple Wallets Privately

Distribute is used to fund bulk wallets. Perfect for airdrops, payroll, and mass payments while maintaining complete privacy.

## How Distribute Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Distribution">
    Set wallet count, SOL range per wallet, and batch name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Wallets">
    System creates recipient wallets and funding address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Backup All Keys">
    Download complete wallet list with private keys.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund Distribution">
    Send total amount to the funding wallet address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Distribution">
    Funds are distributed privately to all generated wallets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Distribution Use Cases

- **Batch Key Export System:** Download complete wallet list with all private keys before funding
- **Session Management**: Resume interrupted distributions and track multiple batches securely
- **Client-Side Encryption**: All batch data and wallet keys encrypted in your browser
- **Recovery Protection**: Access your batch wallets anytime with exported private keys
