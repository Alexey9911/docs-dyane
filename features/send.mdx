## Private Peer-to-Peer Transfers

Send is Ghost Link's core feature for private one-to-one transfers with zero traceability. Perfect for confidential payments and personal transfers where privacy is paramount.

## Supported Assets

Send supports private transfers across multiple assets:
- **SOL** - Native Solana token
- **USDC** - USD Coin stablecoin  
- **USDT** - Tether stablecoin

All assets benefit from the same zero-knowledge privacy protection.

## How Send Works

<Steps>
<Step title="Select Asset and Enter Amount">
Choose SOL, USDC, or USDT, then specify the amount and destination wallet address.
</Step>

<Step title="Backup Recovery Key">
Save the temporary wallet's private key for fund recovery if needed.
</Step>

<Step title="Fund Temporary Wallet">
Send the required amount to the generated funding address.
</Step>

<Step title="Automatic Processing">
The protocol handles deposit, ZK proof generation, and withdrawal.
</Step>

<Step title="Recipient Receives Funds">
The exact amount arrives with no traceable connection to sender.
</Step>
</Steps>
